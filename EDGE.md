### Configuring Microsoft Edge
Open `Microsoft Edge`

Turn off `Help Microsoft improve our products by sending optional diagnostic data about how you use the browser, websites you visit, and crash reports.`

Click `Accept and get started`

Select `Focused`

Click `Confirm`

Click `Sign in to sync data`

Type `your_email`

Click `Other ways to sign in`

Click `Use your password`

Type `your_password`

Click `Next`

Click `Confirm`

Click `Reject All`

Click Page settings icon

Turn off `Show content`

Turn off `Background`
